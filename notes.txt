- Login: POST /auth/token/login/
- Logout: POST /auth/token/logout/

API Endpoints:
- Categories: GET/POST /api/categories/
- Menu Items: GET/POST /api/menu-items/
- Single Menu Item: GET/PUT/PATCH/DELETE /api/menu-items/{id}/
- Cart: GET/POST/DELETE /api/cart/menu-items/
- Orders: GET/POST /api/orders/
- Single Order: GET/PUT/PATCH /api/orders/{id}/
- Managers: GET/POST /api/groups/manager/users/
- Remove Manager: DELETE /api/groups/manager/users/{user_id}/
- Delivery Crew: GET/POST /api/groups/delivery-crew/users/
- Remove Delivery Crew: DELETE /api/groups/delivery-crew/users/{user_id}/
